CHARACTERS OF NOLI ME TANGERE

Main Characters
Name of Character
Chrisostomo Ibarra Maria Clara
Description
Juan Chrisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, also known as Chrisostomo Ibarra, is a young student from San Diego. He returned to his hometown and sought to have a proper school there. His father, Don Rafael Ibarra, is a dear friend of Kapitan Tiago. Maria Clara De Los Santiago, also known as Maria Clara, is the adoptive daughter of Kapitan Tiago. She is the childhood friend of Ibarra. She was sent to study in the convent of Sta. Clara. She also the fiancee of Ibarra in later chapters.


Supporting Characters
Name Of Character
Padre Damaso Kapitan Tiago Donya Victorina
Description
Padre Damaso was a former town curate in San Diego and later transferred. A franciscan priest with a arrogant and brash personality. He is Maria Clara's real father and later opposed her relationship with Ibarra. Santiago De Los Santos, also known as Kapitan Tiago, is a influential businessman and resides in Binondo, Manila. He is the adoptive father of Maria Clara. He struggles to obey and follow the will of the friars. Donya Victorina is a socialite and a friend of Kapitan Tiago. She with her husband, Don Liburcio attended the party held by Kapitan Tiago.
